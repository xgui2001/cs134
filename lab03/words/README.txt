The following databases are helpful for solving word problems (see Lab 3 S20):

dict - an example of a typical unix /usr/share/dict/words, from MacOS

bodyParts - an informal list of parts of the body

clothing - a file of common clothing items from http://www.enchantedlearning.com/wordlist/clothes.shtml

firstNames - a file of common first names from http://www.quietaffiliate.com/free-first-name-and-last-name-databases-csv-and-sql/
   
places - a file of countries and their capitals from http://techslides.com/list-of-countries-and-capitals

cities - cities and towns of 50,00 or more, in the US from https://factfinder.census.gov/faces/tableservices/jsf/pages/productview.xhtml?src=bkmk

diseases - disease related terms from the CDC: https://www.cdc.gov/diseasesconditions/az

bibleNames - a list of names found in the Bible.

frenchCities, italianCities - names of cities from France and Italy that have
	100,000 or more citizens.  From Wikipedia.
